## Script Structure

1. Problem-first hook
   `Digestion trouble? Irregular poops? Fupa?`
   It qualifies the audience immediately with blunt symptom language instead of easing in with education.

2. Immediate emotional release
   `Here's a natural fix.`
   This line is doing more work than it looks like. It flips the user from discomfort to possibility in one beat.

3. Product + promise in one sentence
   `Gut Guardian uses organic plants to help eliminate IBS, bloating, constipation, and more.`
   The ad does not make the viewer wait to learn what the product is for.

4. Bullet-based belief building
   `Smooth digestion... Reduce bloating... Restore gut health... Protect your GI tract...`
   This middle section is classic direct-response structure: short benefit bullets that make the formula feel specific and functional.

5. Trust compression
   `100% Non GMO. Zero sugar. Vegan. ... formulated with nutritionists ... 3rd party tested.`
   The ad compresses multiple objections into one block: ingredients, safety, formulation quality, and validation.

6. Offer-driven close
   `3,000,000+ sipped. Save over 30% today. ... 100% money back guarantee.`
   The final beat stacks social proof, discount urgency, and guarantee to reduce purchase hesitation.

## Why This Structure Works

- It opens with recognition, not explanation.
- It resolves tension quickly with a simple “natural fix” line.
- It avoids a long story arc and goes straight into conversion logic.
- It uses bullet benefits to keep the middle scannable.
- It ends with a dense trust + offer stack instead of a soft CTA.

## What To Borrow For EGUOO

- Start with a symptom stack, not a category statement.
- Add a fast turn line that moves from pain to relief.
- Keep the middle section in short benefit bullets.
- Close with proof, offer, and guarantee in the same final block.
